Who We Are

Our Mission

Healing Rooms host the Presence of Jesus, our Healer, to see people healed, set free, to live a life the Lord has planned for them demonstrating the Kingdom of God.

Our Vision

A loving, and safe environment where people of all walks, receive prayer for physical, mental, and emotional afflictions. We follow the examples of Jesus Christ, who is featured in the Bible, who is the same yesterday today and forever.

A Place of Encounter

Where People Meet the Healer

Every Thursday evening, our trained prayer teams welcome people from all walks of life — no matter their background, belief, or burden. Whether you're carrying physical pain, emotional weight, or mental distress, you are welcomed here.

We believe that Jesus is the same yesterday, today, and forever — and His compassion still moves to heal.

“He heals the brokenhearted and binds up their wounds.”
— Psalm 147:3
Prayer For Every Need

Physical Healing
